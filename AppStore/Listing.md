### Description
LunarGo is your personal astrology and mood tracker designed for teens. Track your emotions, discover cosmic insights based on moon phases, and manifest your dreams with AI-powered daily cosmic cards.

**50+ Features:**

- Daily Cosmic Card -- AI-generated mood card based on moon phase and zodiac
- Moon Phase Tracker -- Automatic moon phase calculation with energy levels
- Mood Check-in -- 5 quick moods with detailed journaling
- Manifestation Board -- Create vision boards with intention tiles
- AI Affirmations -- Personalized daily affirmations
- Insights & Analytics -- Weekly/monthly mood reports
- Moon-Mood Correlation -- Discover how lunar cycles affect your mood
- Social Sharing -- Share mood cards to TikTok and Instagram Stories
- 8 Personalized Themes -- Customizable visual themes for every personality
- Sleep Tracking -- Monitor rest quality correlated with moon phases

## Build Instructions

1. Sync code to MacinCloud
2. Run XcodeGen: `~/tools/xcodegen/bin/xcodegen generate`
3. Build: `xcodebuild build -project LunarGo.xcodeproj -scheme LunarGo -configuration Debug -destination 'platform=iOS Simulator'`
4. Archive via Xcode GUI
5. Distribute via App Store Connect
